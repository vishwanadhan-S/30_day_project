Choosing a Pencil Box

When picking a pencil box, consider:

  • Durability: Will it survive being tossed in a backpack?
  • Capacity: How many items do you need to carry?
  • Compartments: Do you prefer one big space or several organized sections?
  • Style: What design or color do you like?

  • Pencil Works: A broad, slightly formal term for the collection or body of work done in pencil.
