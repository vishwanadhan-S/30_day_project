Classic Pancakes

Ingredients

1 cup all-purpose flour

2 tablespoons sugar

2 cup milk

2 tablespoons salts

2 tablespoons melted butter

Cooking Steps

Mix dry ingredients in a bowl. In another bowl, whisk together wet ingredients. Combine both mixtures and stir until just combined. Cook on a hot griddle until bubbles form, then flip and cook until golden brown.
